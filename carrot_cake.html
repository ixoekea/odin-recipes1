Carrot Cake

Description

This carrot cake uses homemade cream cheese frosting with fresh grated carrots.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners's sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Beat sugar, oil, eggs, and 2 teaspoons vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until well combined.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in carrots. Fold in pecans.
  5. Pour into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 40 minutes. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
  7. To make the frosting: beat butter, cream chese, confectioners' sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
  8. Stir in chopped pecans.
  9. Frost the cooled cake.
